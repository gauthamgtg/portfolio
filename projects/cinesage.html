Overview

CineSage is a comprehensive movie recommendation platform that goes beyond simple filtering. Built with Next.js and Gemini AI, it offers a conversational interface for cinematic exploration, a gamified "Arcade" for movie buffs, and personalized watchlists.

Problem & Solution

Users frequently experience decision paralysis when navigating vast streaming libraries. CineSage solves this by employing an AI-driven discovery engine that interprets natural language queries, allowing users to find films based on specific themes, atmospheres, or niche descriptions that standard algorithms typically overlook.

Technical Architecture

CineSage leverages the **Next.js 14** app router for optimal performance and SEO. The UI is crafted with **Tailwind CSS** for a modern, responsive feel. At its core, **Google Gemini AI** powers the recommendation engine and trivia generation, ensuring dynamic and context-aware interactions.
